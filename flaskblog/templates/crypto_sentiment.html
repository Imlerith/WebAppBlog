Crypto Sentiment

On this page, daily updated sentiment for top cryptocurrencies is presented. The sentiment can be used to predict movements in the related cryptos as well as for research purposes. The methodology behind the sentiment is described in detail in this publication.

The sentiment is constructed via machine learning analysis of all intraday messages from StockTwits related to the selected cryptocurrencies with the largest volume of messages available.
